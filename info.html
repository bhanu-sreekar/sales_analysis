INFROMATION ABOUT THE 5 PLOTS



MONTHLY REVENUE TREND BAR PLOT

This chart represents the total revenue generated in each month. It helps in understanding how sales performance varies over time.

Insight: The highest revenue was recorded in August 2024, indicating a peak sales period. The lowest revenue occurred in March 2024, showing minimal customer activity.


MONTHLY REVENUE GROWTH PATTERN LINE PLOT

This line chart shows the month-wise growth and decline in revenue. It is useful for identifying overall sales trends and seasonality.

Insight: Revenue shows a rising trend from mid-year and reaches a peak in August, followed by a gradual decline towards the end of the year.


ORDERS BY PAYMENT METHOD BAR PLOT

This chart displays the number of orders placed using different payment methods such as UPI, Card, and Cash.

Insight: UPI is the most preferred payment method among customers. Cash payments are comparatively lower, indicating a shift towards digital payments.


REVENUE DISTRIBUTION BY CATEGORY PIE PLOT

This pie chart illustrates how total revenue is distributed across product categories.

Insight: The Electronics category contributes the highest share of revenue. The Accessories category generates comparatively lower revenue.


TOP 5 HIGHEST REVENUE GENERATED CUSTOMERS

This chart highlights the top 5 customers who generated the highest revenue.

Insight: A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue. This indicates high-value customers who are important for business growth.
